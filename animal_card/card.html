Domestic Cat (Kratos)

Taken too early from mother, behaves like a kitten as adult, with "wool sucking" and kneading behaviors.

  • Scientific name: Felis catus
  • Lifespan: 16 years
  • Mass: 3.5 - 7 kg
  • Enemy: vacuum cleaner
  • Quirky habit: sits in the basin and watches people taking bath or using the toilet

The house cat is a small animal with a crazy attitude. Sleeps even 16 hours per day, hunts at night for insects e.g. fly, moth. Cat likes to live in groups and he treats his owner like a member of his pack. Without human care, he quickly goes wild.
